MIXBOXING STATE ASSOCIATION, MAHARASHTRA

MIXBOXING STATE ASSOCIATION, MAHARASHTRA, in short MBSAM, is constituted in the year 2016 and is an association for Mixboxing sports registered under Societies Registration Act in the year 2016. MBSAM is the oldest association in the State and is an apex body for running, promoting & managing Mixboxing in the State of Maharashtra.
