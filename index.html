Odisha Taekwondo Association

Odisha Taekwondo Association, in short OTA, is constituted in the year 1978 and is an association for Taekwondo sports registered under Societies Registration Act in the year 1982. OTA is the oldest association in the State and is an apex body for running, promoting & managing Taekwondo in the State of Odisha.
